Who We Are

At Volunteer Animal Rescue, we are passionate about making a difference in the lives of animals in need. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals, while promoting compassion, education, and advocacy for animal welfare.

Through the dedication of our volunteers, we provide a safe haven for abandoned, abused, and neglected animals, offering them the care and love they deserve. From daily care and fostering to organizing events and raising awareness, our volunteers are the heart of our organization.
